About Us

Welcome to RMR's Homemade Delights — your trusted destination for delicious, homemade Thekua and Salty.

We're a small team (led by me, Ranjan) passionate about bringing the flavors of home to your doorstep. From crispy namkeen to delightful sewai, every product is crafted with care, quality ingredients, and traditional recipes — just like the way our mothers and grandmothers made them.

In a world full of packaged and artificial snacks, we wanted to offer something real Thekua and Snacks made with love, purity, and honesty.
